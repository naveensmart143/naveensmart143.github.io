About

Educational websites can include websites that have topic related resources that act as tool to enhance learning and supplement classroom teaching.These websites help make the process of learning entertaining and interactive to the student especially in today's age.

While there are many advantages of such websites, we also need to be aware of the negatives.Students need to be guided properly. Without proper guidence,students may find resources and content that are not reliable or do not align with the direction of the teaching in the class

Choosing Websites

It is important that we carefully consider what websites to introduce to the students.we need to keep a number of elements in mind while choosing educational websites.The activites on the website should align to what is being thaught in the classroom and the academic level of the students.The website should be appropriate with the age of the students.
